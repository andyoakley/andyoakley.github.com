Taken from Lauren's diary of our trip to South East Asia

Got to Kuwait at about 1am and were told we had to got to a hotel and wait until 12pm for a flight to London. Some people made a fuss and we were put on a Lufthansa flight to Frankfurt. Got there about 8am and then got a flight to Heathrow. Got the bus to Cambridge from Heathrow.
