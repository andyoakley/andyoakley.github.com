My herbs appeared to be growing strong; they germinated quickly and looked to be growing steadily. I moved them to the window so that they'd have their required 6 hours of direct sunlight daily, but things aren't looking great. The sun never shines directly in Seattle.

Although they're not dying as such, I'm not sure they'll ever be edible. Sometimes I feel like I wither and wilt when I don't see daylight for long enough.
