Taken from Lauren's diary of our trip to South East Asia

We had breakfast in the hotel. We sat down in the lounge and tried to sort out some of the days on the holiday as yet unaccounted for. We got a taxi to the airport and at the airport Andy phoned the Cool Point Hotel in the Cameron Highlands and booked two nights. We flew with Thai Airways to Phucket. A man picked us up at the airport and brought us to the Bang Tao Lagoon Bungalows. We had a quaint and quirky little bungalow. We went for a walk on the beach, had a shower and then dinner in the restaurant.
