Taken from Lauren's diary of our trip to South East Asia

We arrived in Bangkok and got a taxi to our hotel, The Ambassador. We got to our room. At about lunch time, we got a taxi to Jim Thompson's house, the house of an American man who started the worldwide trade in Thai silk. It was very nice. We had lunch there and then walked back to our hotel along Sukhumvit Road. We showered and had dinner in the restaurant in the hotel. Bed (very tired, but we seemed to get away with no jetlag).
