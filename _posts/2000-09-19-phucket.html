Taken from Lauren's diary of our trip to South East Asia

We still had burnt and sore feet. Had breakfast. It rained. We watched TV and played chess. Lunch, the weather got nice and we went down and sat on the beach (in the shade). Andy went for a swim in the sea - he has a very big blister on his foot and managed to catch a large jellyfish on his arm. Lauren read her book. Dinner.
