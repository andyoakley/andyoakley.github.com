Taken from Lauren's diary of our trip to South East Asia

We were about to take off at 3.30am when the pilots found a technical problem (the radar didn't work). We waited ages in Singapore airport and at 8am were taken to a hotel in Singapore (the Novotel Apollo). We had lunch and dinner and finally were brought back to the airport for a flight to Kuwait at 10pm. We flew to Kuwait.
