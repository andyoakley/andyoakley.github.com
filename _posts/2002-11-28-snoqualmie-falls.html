Watched (most of) the Thanksgiving Parade through downtown Seattle this morning. Thanksgiving truly does represent the start of the holiday season, and the decorations and festivities were present in force. I remain amazed by the fact that each American high school has a marching band, and they're all really good.

We headed out to Snoqualmie Falls to take in the view and then on up into the pass to the Summit. No snow at all, which is really worrying this late in the year.

Time to catch up on some e-mails.
