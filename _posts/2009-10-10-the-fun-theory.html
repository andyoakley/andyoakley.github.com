In one case turning some stairs that ran alongside an escalator into a human-sized piano increased their usage by some 66 percent. And people enjoyed the additional exercise too. [youtube=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2lXh2n0aPyw] Separately, 'enhancing' a regular park trash can doubled the amount of properly-disposed-of garbage compared to a similar one nearby. [youtube=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cbEKAwCoCKw] The theory: Put the right incentives in place and habits will quickly change accordingly. More on this at thefuntheory.com.
